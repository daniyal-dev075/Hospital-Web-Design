About Us

Dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare with compassion, innovation, and unwavering commitment to our community.

Our Story

A Legacy of Care

Founded in 2001, Modern Care Hospital began as a small clinic with a big dream: to make world-class healthcare accessible to everyone. Today, we are a leading medical institution equipped with cutting-edge technology and a team of internationally renowned specialists.

But despite our growth, our core philosophy remains unchanged: every patient is treated like family. We believe that true healing happens when advanced medicine is combined with genuine human warmth.

Our Mission

To deliver unparalleled, compassionate healthcare services that improve the quality of life for our patients, while fostering a culture of continuous medical innovation and community well-being.

Our Vision

To be the global benchmark for patient-centered healthcare, where medical excellence meets deep empathy, shaping a healthier and brighter future for all.

Milestones

Our Journey

2001

Modern Care Clinic was founded by Dr. Sarah Jenkins with just 10 beds and a focus on community health.

2010

Expanded into a fully equipped 150-bed hospital, introducing the region's first advanced Cardiology Center.

2018

Awarded National Accreditation for Excellence in Healthcare Quality and Patient Safety.

2026

Launch of our AI-assisted diagnostic wing and state-of-the-art robotic surgery department.
