Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Modern Care Hospital ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and securing your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (Name, DOB, Social Security Number)
  • Contact information (Email, Phone number, Address)
  • Medical history and health records (Protected Health Information - PHI)
  • Insurance and billing details

3. HIPAA Compliance

All Protected Health Information (PHI) is handled in strict compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Your medical data is encrypted and stored securely.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@moderncare.com.
