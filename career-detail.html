Registered Nurse - ICU

Full-time • Main Campus • Night Shift

Role Overview

We are seeking a dedicated and compassionate ICU Registered Nurse to join our intensive care team. You will be responsible for closely monitoring patients with life-threatening conditions, operating life-support systems, and collaborating with a multidisciplinary medical team.

Requirements

  • Valid State RN License
  • Minimum 2 years of critical care experience
  • BLS and ACLS Certification
  • Excellent communication and critical thinking skills

Benefits

  • Comprehensive Health, Dental, and Vision Insurance
  • 401(k) with company match
  • Tuition Reimbursement
  • Generous PTO and paid holidays
