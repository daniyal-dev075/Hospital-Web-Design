Overview

The Modern Care Hospital Cardiology Center is a premier facility dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of heart disease. Our multidisciplinary team of board-certified cardiologists, cardiovascular surgeons, and specialized nurses work collaboratively to provide personalized care plans for every patient.

We are equipped with state-of-the-art Catheterization Labs and advanced non-invasive imaging technologies, ensuring that our patients receive the most accurate diagnoses and effective treatments available today.

Conditions We Treat

  • Coronary Artery Disease
  • Heart Failure
  • Arrhythmias (Atrial Fibrillation)
  • Valvular Heart Disease
  • Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)
  • Peripheral Artery Disease
  • Congenital Heart Defects
  • Aortic Aneurysms

Advanced Treatments & Procedures

We offer a full spectrum of cardiovascular procedures, prioritizing minimally invasive options whenever possible to reduce recovery time.

  • Angioplasty and Stenting: Minimally invasive procedures to open blocked arteries.
  • Electrophysiology Studies: Diagnosis and treatment of abnormal heart rhythms, including pacemakers and ablation.
  • TAVR (Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement): Minimally invasive valve replacement for high-risk patients.
  • Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG): Advanced surgical interventions for severe blockages.
