Biography

Dr. Sarah Jenkins is an internationally recognized interventional cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in diagnosing and treating complex cardiovascular diseases. She completed her fellowship at Johns Hopkins and has since pioneered several minimally invasive techniques for valve replacement.

She is deeply passionate about preventive cardiology and runs a specialized clinic focusing on heart health in women.
