Frequently Asked Questions

What are your visiting hours? +
General visiting hours are from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. Intensive Care Units (ICU) have restricted hours from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM. Only two visitors are allowed per patient at a time.
Do I need a referral to see a specialist? +
It depends on your insurance provider. Many HMO plans require a referral from your primary care physician, while PPO plans usually do not. Please check with your insurance provider or call our billing department for clarification.
How do I get a copy of my medical records? +
You can access most of your test results and visit summaries instantly through our Patient Portal. For complete official records, please submit a Medical Records Request form online or visit the records department on the ground floor.
