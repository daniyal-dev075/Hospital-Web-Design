Insurance & Billing

We strive to make the billing process as transparent and easy as possible. Here is everything you need to know about payments and insurance coverage.

Accepted Insurance Plans

We accept most major local and international health insurance plans, including:

  • BlueCross BlueShield
  • Aetna
  • Cigna
  • UnitedHealthcare
  • Medicare & Medicaid

*Please verify your coverage directly with your provider prior to your visit.

Pay Your Bill Online

Securely pay your hospital and physician bills online using our payment portal. You will need your statement ID and patient account number.

Financial Assistance

Modern Care Hospital provides financial assistance to patients who are uninsured or underinsured. Contact our financial counselors to see if you qualify.
