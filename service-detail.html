Diagnostic Imaging Center

Precision Diagnostics

Our imaging center is equipped with the latest technology to provide highly detailed images, allowing our doctors to make accurate and timely diagnoses. We offer a full suite of radiological services in a comfortable and safe environment.

Available Scans

  • 3T MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging)
  • 128-Slice CT Scan
  • Digital X-Rays
  • 3D Mammography
  • DEXA Bone Density Scan

Prepare for your visit

Depending on your scan, you may need to fast or avoid certain medications. Please consult the instructions sent to your Patient Portal prior to your appointment.
